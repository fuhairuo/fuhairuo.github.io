# Isotope Geochemistry
------

I conduct clean-lab chemistry procedures and high-precision isotopic analysis using multicollector ICP-MS to investigate the chemical and thermal evolution of planetary systems.

# Paleomagnetism
------
I combine field sampling, rock-magnetic characterization, and laboratory paleomagnetic measurements to reconstruct plate motions and supercontinental evolution through deep time.
